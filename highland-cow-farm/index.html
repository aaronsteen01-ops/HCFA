Highland Cow Farm Adventure

Welcome to your cosy Highland hideaway. Tend to your fluffy herd, keep them happy, and earn charming farm treasures through daily mini-adventures.

How to Play

Each day you will complete four brisk mini-games: keep runaway cows safe, match the right feed, brush away the burrs, and lead a lively ceilidh dance. Happy cows grant more rewards. Watch the chonk level – a little extra fluff is adorable, but too much means slower cows tomorrow!
